# Xafsizyol — Project Overview

## What is Xafsizyol?

Xafsizyol (means "Safe Road" in Uzbek) is a free civic platform for reporting road potholes and hazards across Uzbekistan. Citizens use the app to submit reports about road damage, and road authorities use it to track and manage repairs.

## How It Works

1. A citizen notices a pothole or road hazard
2. They open Xafsizyol and submit a report with a photo and GPS location
3. The report appears on the public map instantly
4. Road authorities review the report and update its status
5. The citizen receives a Telegram notification when the status changes
6. Once repaired, the report is marked as Fixed

## Platforms

- **Telegram Mini App** — opens directly inside Telegram, no separate browser or app install needed
- **Web browser** — also accessible as a regular website
- Both platforms share the same data and features

## Languages Supported

- O'zbek (Uzbek)
- Русский (Russian)
- English

## Who Can Use It?

Xafsizyol is completely free for all citizens of Uzbekistan. Anyone who drives, walks, or bikes and notices road hazards can submit a report. No registration required — it uses your Telegram account automatically.

## Region

All of Uzbekistan — Tashkent city and all provinces (viloyatlar):
Toshkent viloyati, Samarqand, Buxoro, Namangan, Farg'ona, Andijon, Qashqadaryo, Surxondaryo, Xorazm, Navoiy, Jizzax, Sirdaryo, Qoraqalpog'iston Respublikasi.

## Why Xafsizyol Matters

- Road potholes cause millions of soums in vehicle damage every year in Uzbekistan (tires, suspension, rims, wheel alignment)
- Centralized reporting gives authorities data to prioritize repairs
- Voting shows which problems affect the most people
- Transparent status tracking builds trust between citizens and authorities
- The more people report, the faster roads get fixed
