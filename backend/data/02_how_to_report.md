# How to Report a Road Problem

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1 — Open the App
- In Telegram: press the "Muammoni xabar qilish" (Report a problem) button in the Xafsizyol bot
- On web: go to the Xafsizyol website and click the "+" button

### Step 2 — Take a Photo
- Take a photo of the pothole or road hazard (REQUIRED — you cannot submit without a photo)
- Or choose an existing photo from your gallery
- The photo is used to verify the problem is real
- Photos are automatically compressed before upload

### Step 3 — Confirm Your Location
- Your GPS location is detected automatically
- A mini map appears showing your location with a pin in the center
- Move the map to adjust the pin to the exact spot of the pothole
- Press the navigation button (bottom right of map) to re-center on your GPS position
- You can also type an address to search for a specific location
- City and district fields are auto-filled from your location

### Step 4 — Choose Severity and Add Details
- Select severity level: Small / Medium / Critical
- Enter your phone number (optional — so authorities can contact you)
- Write a short description of the problem (optional but helpful)

### Step 5 — Preview and Submit
- Review all details before submitting
- Press "Yuborish" (Submit) to send the report
- Your report appears on the public map instantly

## Important Notes

- Photo is REQUIRED — the report cannot be submitted without a photo
- Phone number is OPTIONAL — only needed if you want authorities to contact you
- Location must be confirmed on the map
- After submission you receive a confirmation and can track the report in "My Reports"

## After Submitting

- The report gets "Pending" status immediately
- You receive a Telegram notification when authorities update the status
- You can see your report in the "My Reports" section
- Other users can vote on your report if they have the same problem
